# Leverage Analysis

Delegate to `strategist` subagent.

Read `.claude/skills/case-strategy/SKILL.md`.

Workflow:

1. Map your leverage:
   - Evidence (documentary, testimonial, expert, demonstrative)
   - Legal theories with strength assessment
   - Forum advantages
   - Time and runway
   - Specific facts adverse to opposing party
   - Public records constraining their narrative
   - Money, health, capacity
   - Counsel available

2. Map opposing party's leverage:
   - Institutional resources
   - Internal documents only they have
   - Continuity of personnel
   - Political backing
   - Insurance defense
   - Precedential favorability
   - Possession of the child (in CPS cases — the most powerful)

3. Identify asymmetries:
   - Where do you have leverage they can't match?
   - Where do they have leverage you can't overcome?
   - What leverage is time-decaying (theirs and yours)?
   - What leverage can be created (new evidence, new claims, new forums, new alliances)?

4. Output:
   - Two-column leverage map (yours / theirs)
   - Asymmetries with significance
   - Time-decay analysis
   - Leverage-creation opportunities ranked by feasibility and impact

5. Save to `08-research/leverage-analysis-<YYYYMMDD>.md`.

6. **GUARDRAIL**: Present the map. Do not recommend specific exploitation. The user decides.

Log in `_session-log.md`.

Run leverage analysis before major decision points: settlement discussions, filing decisions, forum selection.
