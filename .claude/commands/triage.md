# Triage — Immediate Priorities

Apply Prompt 7 (Immediate Priorities Triage) from the Prompt Library.

Read the session log, master index, and chronology to understand current case state.

Produce a prioritized action list:

TODAY (next 24 hours): No more than 5 items.
THIS WEEK: 5-10 items.
THIS MONTH: 10-15 items.

For each action:
- The action (specific and concrete)
- Why it matters
- How long it should take
- Who I need to contact / what I need to obtain
- What I will have at the end

Identify the 3 highest-leverage actions where the user's time will most affect the outcome.

Distinguish: emotional regulation vs. strategic vs. legal vs. documentation actions.

If a deadline is within 72 hours and is approaching unprepared, surface it FIRST regardless of other priorities.

Save to `08-research/triage-<YYYYMMDD>.md`.

Log in `_session-log.md`.
