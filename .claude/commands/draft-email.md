# Draft Case Email

Apply Prompt 21 (Caseworker Email Drafter) from the Prompt Library.

Recipient and topic: $ARGUMENTS

Produce:

1. SUBJECT LINE (specific, dated, dispositive)
2. BODY (3-4 paragraphs maximum; firm, professional, not subservient)
3. CALL TO ACTION (specific, with deadline)
4. CC LINE (who else should be on this; usually supervisor for substantive items)
5. ATTACHMENTS (what should be attached)
6. FOLLOW-UP PLAN (what I do if they don't respond by [date])

Assume the email will be Exhibit X in court. Tone must be professional, fact-anchored, free of language that can be characterized as hostile, threatening, or unstable.

Save to `07-drafts/correspondence/email-<recipient>-<YYYYMMDD>.md`.

Log in `_session-log.md`.
