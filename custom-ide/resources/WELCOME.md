# Welcome to HolyClaude IDE

Your specialized environment for child welfare litigation and AI-driven evidence management.

## Getting Started

1.  **AI Sidepane:** Click the **Star Icon** in the left activity bar to open HolyClaude.
2.  **Highlight & Refactor:** Select any text in the editor and ask HolyClaude to refactor or analyze it.
3.  **Direct Apply:** When HolyClaude suggests code, click the **Apply** button to update your document instantly.
4.  **Evidence Vault:** Use the file explorer to manage your `05-evidence` and `07-drafts`.

---
*HolyClaude is powered by the CHIPS Copilot framework.*
